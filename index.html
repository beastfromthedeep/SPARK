SPARK

Solar Particle Acceleration, Radiation and Kinetics

An ESA M-class mission concept addressing energetic processes in solar flares

1. Executive Summary

SPARK (Solar Particle Acceleration, Radiation and Kinetics) is a proposed ESA M-class mission dedicated to resolving how magnetic energy is rapidly converted into energetic particles and radiation during solar flares.

2. Mission Overview

SPARK combines high-cadence EUV imaging with hard X-ray and gamma-ray spectroscopy to directly link magnetic reconnection, plasma heating, and particle acceleration at their sites of origin.

3. Mission Status

SPARK is currently under development as a candidate ESA M-class mission concept.
